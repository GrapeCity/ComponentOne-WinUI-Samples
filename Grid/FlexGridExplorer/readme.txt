FlexGridExplorer for WinUI
------------------------------------------
Shows samples of the FlexGrid control.

Shows the following samples:

	- GettingStarted
	- ColumnDefinitions
	- SelectionModes
	- EditConfirmation
	- EditingForm
	- ConditionalFormatting
	- CustomCells
	- Grouping
	- RowDetails
	- FullTextFilter
	- FilterRow
	- ColumnLayout
	- StarSizing
	- CellFreezing
	- CustomMerging
	- Unbound
	- OnDemand
	- CustomAppearance
	- NewRow
	- CheckList
	- Paging
	- Financial
	- LiveUpdates
	- CustomSortIcon
    - MouseHover
